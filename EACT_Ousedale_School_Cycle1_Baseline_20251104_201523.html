Interpretation Guide

Mean Scores: Statements range from 1-5. Scores above 4.0 indicate strong agreement, scores between 3.0-4.0 show moderate agreement, and scores below 3.0 suggest areas where students lack confidence or need additional support.

Variance: Measures how spread out student responses are. Low variance (below 0.8) means most students agree and responses are consistent - ideal for building on strengths or addressing common challenges. High variance (above 1.5) indicates mixed responses where some students strongly agree while others don't - suggesting differentiated support may be needed. Moderate variance (0.8-1.5) shows typical spread in student experiences.
